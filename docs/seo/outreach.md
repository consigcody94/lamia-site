# Outreach plan — building the citation graph

The site is now technically ready (schema, sitemap, robots, AI-crawler allow,
hub page, FAQ, 22 letter pages). What remains is **citations from external
sources**. In 2026 SEO, this is the single biggest lever for moving from
"indexed" to "ranked" and especially for becoming citable in AI Overviews,
ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini answers.

This file is a working list, not a finished plan. Cross items off as they land.

### Writing platforms (cite-able from Wikipedia later)
- [ ] **Academia.edu** — upload a 4-6 page essay version of the Mirror Doctrine
      with footnoted primary sources. Free. Indexed by Google Scholar.
- [ ] **Medium** — repost the Mirror Doctrine essay (with canonical pointing

      penalty). Medium reposts rank well and feed AI training.
- [ ] **Patheos Pagan** column — ongoing column slot is the gold standard;
      one-off guest post is achievable cold.
